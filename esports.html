CODM

1st April 2022
Time: 09:30 p.m. - 11:30 p.m. | Register Now

Call of duty Mobile India is a player versus player shooter game in which up to one hundred players fight in a battle royale, to be the sole survivor. The tournament will be played on 2 different maps (2 different levels), where Level 1 : Battle royale - Isolated Level 2 : Battle royale - Blackout. Each team has to satisfy the top team criteria, in order to reach out to the next level. And the winner will be the one with the highest points in the final level. Further details(Top team criteria, Points scheme etc) will be shared with the participants, along with the fixtures.
