War of Words - Debate

1st April 2022
Time: 04:00 p.m. - 07:00 p.m. | Register Now

Interaction, discussion, and debating is the key to progress. When you claim, agree, argue, and refute, a whole dynamics of questions unfold. But the progression of debate thirsts a stage to rise. So, here to enhance your ability to think, research, and speak, DebSoc has come up with the War Of Words. This would be a very similar event as that which was done 2 years back. It will not only help you in developing your presentation skills, but it's also going to be very interesting to have counter ideas on given themes. Organizing your thoughts, researching, and presenting is going to be a personality booster for you.

  • Mode: offline
  • Rules:
    • There will be a team of three, i.e. two students and one professor.
    • Students will have to go and look for a professor to speak to them.
    • Each team will get around two mins to speak.
    • The event will be for 3 hrs.
    • There will be two rounds.
    • The theme would revolve around culture, which includes historical events, pop culture, etc.
    • Results will be announced on our Instagram handle.
