Battle of Bands

3rd April 2022
Time: 05:00 p.m. - 07:00 p.m. | Register Now

Battle of Bands is where battalions of music enthusiasts come together and strum their vocal cords against one another in a fierce battle to claim the glory of victory. Spectators will witness a plethora of notes each competing against the other to capture the hearts of dazzled listeners.

  • Mode: Offline
  • Venue: Football Ground
  • Rules:
    • A band must have a maximum of 8 and a minimum of 3 members.
    • The performance should not exceed 15 minutes.
    • Original compositions are allowed and appreciated.
    • Music - We believe in art beyond boundaries. All genres and languages are accepted.
    • There would be a penalty for exceeding the time limit.
    • Points will be awarded on the basis of creativity, soundtrack, originality, and lyrics.
    • The deadline for the registrations is 11:59 PM on 31st March 2022.
